Who I Am
Where technical depth
meets product instinct.

I'm Jaskiran Kaur — Jazz — a product thinker who's spent six years in the thick of enterprise software, fintech workflows, and large-scale operations. At Adobe, I worked as a Technical Consultant, bridging the gap between complex software capabilities and real business outcomes for enterprise clients. That experience taught me one thing early: the best product decisions aren't made in Jira — they're made by deeply understanding the person on the other end of the screen.

During my Program Manager internship at Dhanivesh App, I wrote PRDs, mapped end-to-end user journeys, and ran competitive research for a fintech product. I saw how the smallest friction in a financial flow could erode trust faster than any bug. That's where my love for product craft crystallised — the rigour, the empathy, the relentless prioritisation.

At Teleperformance, I led teams and managed operations at scale — the kind of role that forces you to think in systems, not in silos. Pair that with an MBA in International Business, and you get a PM candidate who can hold a roadmap conversation with engineering, a business case conversation with leadership, and a user research conversation with customers — all in the same afternoon.

I'm now actively transitioning into Product Management, targeting roles in India and the UAE/MEA region. My north star: ship products that solve real problems for real people, backed by data and driven by curiosity.
